This is a little known secret of Sublime Text - the ability to have multiple items in your clipboard history and access them with the keyboard.

To access the clipboard history, simply hit <code>⌘</code> + <code>K</code>, <code>⌘</code> + <code>V</code> - so just add the <code>⌘</code> + <code>K</code> infront of your regular paste shortcut. Windows users swap out <code>⌘</code> with <code>Ctrl</code>.
